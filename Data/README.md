# Data

All source datasets used in this study are publicly accessible through Google Earth Engine or official institutional repositories, including Landsat surface reflectance and thermal imagery, the 1-km station-based gridded precipitation and potential evapotranspiration dataset for the Qinghai-Tibetan Plateau from the National Tibetan Plateau Data Center, MERIT DEM, the CSIC global SPEI database, TerraClimate, and GLDAS-2. Field soil-moisture measurements collected in August 2024 are available from the corresponding author upon reasonable request.
